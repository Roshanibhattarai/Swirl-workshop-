Some Korean Foods I Like

Kimchi

Kimchi is a traditional S.Korea's food with fegmented vegetabless it has a yummy taste.


Bibimbap

Bibimbap is a mixed rice dish with vegetables, meat, and a fried egg on top.It is often served with a spicy gochujang sauce.


Ramyeon

Ramyeon is a popular Korean instant noodle dish that is faster and easier to prepare. It consists of noodles, a seasoning packet, andsome optional things like vegetables or meat.
